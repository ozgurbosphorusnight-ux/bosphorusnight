When you contact us via WhatsApp or through our website, we may collect the following information:

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for:

  • Processing and confirming your cruise reservation

We may share your information with our cruise operator partners solely for the purpose of fulfilling your reservation. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Cookies

Our website may use minimal cookies for functionality purposes (such as language preference). We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.
