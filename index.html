Your Experience

What's Included

Bosphorus Night Cruise

3.5-hour cruise with iconic Istanbul views

Sail past the Maiden's Tower, Dolmabahçe Palace, Bosphorus Bridge, Beylerbeyi Palace, Ortaköy Mosque, and Rumeli Fortress. Departs from Kabataş at 20:45, returns at 23:30.

Full Dinner Service

7 meze varieties, main course & dessert

7 types of meze, choice of fish, chicken, or meatball main course, and dessert. Vegetarian menu available. VIP packages include mixed grill platter and premium meat options.

Live Entertainment

Live singer, DJ & Turkish night dances

Live singer performances, traditional Turkish night show with folk dances, belly dance, and DJ music. Entertainment runs until 21:00.

Hotel Transfer

Optional pickup & drop-off from your hotel

Round-trip hotel pickup and drop-off available from Sultanahmet, Taksim, and Beyoğlu areas. Available as an add-on when booking. Mention your hotel location on WhatsApp.

7/24 Support

Instant WhatsApp support anytime

We're with you before, during, and after your cruise. Message or call us on WhatsApp anytime — we respond instantly, 7 days a week, 24 hours a day.

Special Occasions

Romantic table, proposal & birthday packages

Celebrating something special? We offer romantic table setup with candles & flowers (+€25), proposal package with decoration, photographer & cake (+€50), and birthday celebrations. Tell us on WhatsApp!

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

All packages include a full dinner with 7 varieties of Turkish meze, your choice of main course (fish, chicken, or meatball), and dessert. VIP packages feature a premium mixed grill platter with selected cuts. Vegetarian options are available on request.

All packages include soft drinks. You can add an unlimited alcohol package for just +€10 per person. This can be arranged during booking on WhatsApp.

Cruises depart from Kabataş pier at 20:45 and return to the same location at 23:30 (3-hour cruise). The exact meeting point will be shared on WhatsApp after booking.

The cruise sails along the Bosphorus Strait, passing by iconic landmarks including Dolmabahçe Palace, Bosphorus Bridge, Maiden's Tower, Ortaköy Mosque, and more. You'll see Istanbul's stunning skyline illuminated at night from both the European and Asian sides.

It depends on your package. Premium tables are closer to the stage, and VIP tables have the best location with an unobstructed view. If stage proximity is important to you, let us know on WhatsApp and we'll arrange the best available spot.

The cruise features live singer performances, a traditional Turkish Night show with folk dances, belly dance, and DJ music. Entertainment runs until 21:00, followed by a relaxing return cruise.

Yes! Same-day bookings are available until 20:00 (1 hour before departure). Just message us on WhatsApp and we'll check availability instantly.

No prepayment is required. You pay directly on the boat before or during the cruise. We accept cash (EUR, TRY, USD) and credit cards.

Yes! Hotel transfer is available as an optional add-on for +€15 per person. This includes both pickup from and drop-off to your hotel. Arrange it easily during booking on WhatsApp.

Yes. Free cancellation is available up to 2 hours before the cruise (until 19:00). After that, please let us know on WhatsApp so we can assist you. Since there's no prepayment, the process is hassle-free.

The meeting point depends on which cruise you book. After your reservation is confirmed, we'll send you the exact location with a Google Maps pin on WhatsApp, along with clear directions.
